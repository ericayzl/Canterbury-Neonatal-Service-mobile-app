Support for Parents

Canterbury Neonatal Service ensure the multi-disciplinary team advocates for the baby/pēpi and their family/whānau in all aspects of care, including complaints. Our care promote and foster healthy interpersonal relationships between nga mātua/parents and baby/pēpi.

Patient Advocacy Service

The patient advocacy service is independent of the Canterbury District Health Board. It is a free service and provides:

  • Information on Health and Disability Consumer Rights.
  • Assistance to answer questions and resolve difficulties.

Help and Advice

Some people can help you through this stressful time with your new baby. Please ask us about these services:

  • Dietitian
  • Infant Feeding Specialist
  • Lactation Consultants
  • Neonatal Trust
  • Photo Board
  • Physiotherapist
  • Social Workers
  • Speech Language Therapists

Baby Friendly Hospital Initiative

The Neonatal Service supports the Baby Friendly Initiative and recognises the Ten Steps to Successful Breastfeeding.

For information about the Ten Steps and the Baby Friendly Hospital Initiative, see babyfriendly.org.nz.
